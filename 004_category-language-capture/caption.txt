Principle Nº 4: Category Language Capture

Whoever defines the language defines the market. If you own the vocabulary of the problem, every conversation in the market reinforces your category leadership.

Ask yourself: What's the phrase you want your customers to use to describe what you do, instead of a generic label a competitor could also claim?
