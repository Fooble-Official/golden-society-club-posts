Principle Nº 21: Interface Economics

Value is often lost at the handoff, not in the function itself. Specialized teams can each be excellent and still produce mediocrity if the seams between them are weak.

Ask yourself: In your business's workflow, which handoff loses the most context or time? How do you compress or remove it?
