Principle Nº 22: Incentive Gradient Design

People move in the direction of the reward gradient, not the mission statement. If your mission says one thing and your bonus structure rewards another, people follow the bonus.

Ask yourself: What behaviors are your business's current incentives actually rewarding, and are those the ones you want?
