Principle Nº 28: Internal Market Making

Scarce internal resources should flow toward highest-value uses, not the loudest politics. Scarce resources need a pricing mechanism or they get allocated by whoever complains loudest.

Ask yourself: When two priorities in your business compete for the same time, cash, or attention, what's the rule for who wins?
