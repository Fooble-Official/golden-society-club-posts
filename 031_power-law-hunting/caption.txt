Principle Nº 31: Power-Law Hunting

In many domains, a few outcomes drive most of the returns. Managing everything like a normal distribution causes you to miss the fat tail where real value lives.

Ask yourself: Where in your business are you managing for averages when you should be hunting for the one 100x outcome?
