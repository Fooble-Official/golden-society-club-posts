Principle Nº 32: Variance Strategy

You should not eliminate all variance; you should choose where to absorb and where to suppress it. The mistake is treating all variance as bad.

Ask yourself: Where in your business should you invite more variance (marketing tests)? Where should you kill it (payouts)?
