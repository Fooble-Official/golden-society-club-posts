Principle Nº 33: Asymmetric Bet Design

The best bets have limited downside and nonlinear upside. Capped downside + uncapped upside = the shape of wealth creation.

Ask yourself: Of your next 3 business bets, which is most asymmetric? Are you overweighting the symmetric ones?
