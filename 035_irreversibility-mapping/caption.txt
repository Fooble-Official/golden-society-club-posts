Principle Nº 35: Irreversibility Mapping

Some decisions can be undone cheaply; others permanently narrow the future. Speed on reversible decisions, slowness on irreversible ones.

Ask yourself: For your next big business decision, is it a one-way door or a two-way door? Are you treating it accordingly?
