Principle Nº 38: Constraint Arbitrage

Constraints can be exploited strategically when others treat them only as limitations. Local, regulated, or niche constraints are moats in disguise.

Ask yourself: What's a market-specific constraint (licensing, geography, culture) that competitors hate, but you can weaponize?
