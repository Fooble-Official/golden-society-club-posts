Principle Nº 48: Counterparty Concentration Risk

Dependence on a few relationships makes the business more brittle than top-line revenue suggests. Big wins feel like traction and are actually concentration.

Ask yourself: What percentage of your revenue or users comes from your top 5 customers or partners, and is that concentration rising or falling?
