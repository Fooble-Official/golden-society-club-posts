Principle Nº 50: Liquidity Stress Design

Insolvency often happens through timing, not lack of theoretical profitability. Profitable companies die from cash timing mismatches.

Ask yourself: If your business revenue dropped 40% for 3 months, how many weeks could you operate before cutting?
