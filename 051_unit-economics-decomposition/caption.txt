Principle Nº 51: Unit-Economics Decomposition

Overall economics are too blunt; you need to know exactly which sub-components create or destroy value. Good aggregate numbers can hide bad underlying economics.

Ask yourself: Can you name every layer of cost between a business signup and a profit dollar? What's the biggest surprise?
