Principle Nº 57: Revenue Quality Analysis

A dollar of revenue is not equal to every other dollar of revenue. Chasing any revenue is beginner; chasing high-quality revenue is operator.

Ask yourself: What percentage of your business's projected revenue is recurring, high-margin, diversified, and cash-reliable?
