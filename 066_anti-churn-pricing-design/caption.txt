Principle Nº 66: Anti-Churn Pricing Design

Pricing should reduce regret after purchase. The charge that feels reasonable stays; the one that feels painful churns.

Ask yourself: Does a member who used your app 0 times this month feel guilty, angry, or OK with the charge?
