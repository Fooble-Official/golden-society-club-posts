Principle Nº 75: Multi-Player Coordination Incentives

Systems fail when participants are not aligned in timing or benefit. If any party has to wait, they'll leak.

Ask yourself: Do all the different parties in your business model, suppliers, customers, partners, win at the same moment, or does one side always have to wait?
