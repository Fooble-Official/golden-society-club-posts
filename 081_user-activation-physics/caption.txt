Principle Nº 81: User Activation Physics

Users don't stay because they signed up, they stay because they felt value fast. Every day a new user goes without their "aha" moment raises the probability they churn.

Ask yourself: What's your business's "aha moment," and how fast does a new user hit it? Can you make it faster?
