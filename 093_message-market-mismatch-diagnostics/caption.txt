Principle Nº 93: Message-Market Mismatch Diagnostics

Low conversion often means misalignment, not lack of demand. Before giving up, diagnose: message, market, or offer.

Ask yourself: If your business's traffic is high but conversion is low, is the message wrong, the offer wrong, or the audience wrong?
