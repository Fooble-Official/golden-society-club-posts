Principle Nº 94: Audience Contamination

Algorithms learn from your inputs, even bad ones. Bad leads don't just waste money today; they train the algorithm to bring more bad leads tomorrow.

Ask yourself: Is your business's ad targeting teaching Meta or TikTok to find real customers, or tourists who look similar?
