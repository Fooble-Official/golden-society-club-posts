Principle Nº 98: Intent Harvesting

The best conversions happen when intent is already forming. Catching intent while it's forming is 10x cheaper than selling once it's explicit and contested.

Ask yourself: What early intent signals, searches, behaviors, life events, predict a customer is about to want your business?
