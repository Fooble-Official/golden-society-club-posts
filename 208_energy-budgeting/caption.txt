Principle Nº 208: Energy Budgeting

Schedule tasks by energy, not time. Two hours of work aren't interchangeable just because they're both 'an hour.' The same task takes less effort and produces a better result when it's matched to a high-energy window, and more effort for a worse result in a low one.

Ask yourself: Are you protecting your peak-energy hours from low-value work?
