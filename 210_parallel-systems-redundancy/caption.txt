Principle Nº 210: Parallel Systems (Redundancy)

Backups for critical elements. A backup only adds real reliability if its failure is independent from the original, two systems that both depend on the same server, the same person's judgment, or the same supplier's warehouse aren't actually redundant, they're the same point of failure wearing two names.

Ask yourself: What critical thing has no backup right now?
