Principle Nº 211: Learning Loops

Treat failures as data. The gap between people who improve fast and people who stay stuck usually isn't talent, it's whether the failure got turned into a specific, written adjustment before the feeling faded.

Ask yourself: What recent failure have you not actually learned from yet?
