Principle Nº 214: Parallel Processing (Batching)

Group similar tasks. Task switching doesn't just cost the seconds it takes to open a new tab, it costs the minutes it takes your attention to fully re-enter the task you left, a cost that's invisible because it doesn't show up as a line item anywhere.

Ask yourself: What are you doing scattered that you could batch into one block?
