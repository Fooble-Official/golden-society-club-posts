Principle Nº 215: Minimum Effective Dose

Do just enough. Effort and result don't rise together forever, past a certain point, extra effort buys smaller and smaller improvements while the cost stays constant or climbs.

Ask yourself: Where are you doing too much to hit a result you could get with less?
