Principle Nº 217: Consistent Rest & Recovery

Schedule recovery like work. Performance isn't just a function of how much stress you can handle, it's a function of stress relative to the recovery you've actually built in, and those two have to move together.

Ask yourself: Are you treating rest as earned luxury, or non-negotiable input?
