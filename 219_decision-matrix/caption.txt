Principle Nº 219: Decision Matrix

Structured criteria for complex choices. Gut feeling isn't wrong so much as it's unweighted, it lets whichever factor is loudest in the moment (price, excitement, fear) dominate the decision, even when that factor isn't actually the one that matters most to you.

Ask yourself: What big decision should you run through a matrix instead of vibes?
