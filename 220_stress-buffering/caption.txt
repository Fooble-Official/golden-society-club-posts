Principle Nº 220: Stress Buffering

Train autonomic system to switch states. Resilience isn't the same as calmness, a person who's calm but can't access urgency when a real deadline hits isn't actually flexible, just stuck in one gear.

Ask yourself: Are you stuck in one nervous system state most of the time?
