Principle Nº 222: Priming (Behavioral Nudges)

Subtle cues trigger action. A cue only works as a nudge if two things are both true: it's noticeable, and the action it points to is easy to complete right there.

Ask yourself: What's the subtlest cue you could plant to trigger a desired action?
