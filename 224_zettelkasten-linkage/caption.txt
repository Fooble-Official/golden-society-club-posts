Principle Nº 224: Zettelkasten Linkage

Connect notes to build knowledge web. A note sitting alone can only tell you what it already says, it can't surprise you, because it has no relationship to anything else you know.

Ask yourself: Do your notes connect, or are they a graveyard of isolated thoughts?
