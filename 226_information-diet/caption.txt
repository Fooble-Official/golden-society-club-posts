Principle Nº 226: Information Diet

Curate inputs. Insight doesn't come from volume of consumption, it comes from quality multiplied by the time you actually give it, which means a mediocre source you check five times a day can crowd out a genuinely good one you only have time for once a week.

Ask yourself: What's in your information diet that's pure junk, but you consume daily?
