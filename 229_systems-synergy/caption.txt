Principle Nº 229: Systems Synergy

Integrate subsystems for compound effect. Running multiple systems side by side doesn't automatically create synergy, two well-designed but unrelated routines just add up to their sum, nothing more.

Ask yourself: Where can you stack systems so one action serves multiple goals?
