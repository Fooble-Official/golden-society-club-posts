Principle Nº 230: Boundaries of Control

Focus on what you can control. Effort isn't automatically productive just because it's effort, the same energy spent on something you can't control produces nothing, no matter how hard you push, while the same energy spent on something you can control compounds.

Ask yourself: What are you worrying about that you can't actually control?
