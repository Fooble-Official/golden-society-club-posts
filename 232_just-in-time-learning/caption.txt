Principle Nº 232: Just-In-Time Learning

Learn skills when needed. Learning ahead of need feels responsible, but most of it doesn't survive contact with actually needing it later, the brain deprioritizes information it can't immediately use.

Ask yourself: What are you 'preparing to learn' instead of just learning by doing?
