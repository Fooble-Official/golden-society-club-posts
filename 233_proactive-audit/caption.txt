Principle Nº 233: Proactive Audit

Regularly inspect and fine-tune systems. Systems don't usually fail all at once, they drift, a small deviation at a time, and each individual drift feels too small to bother fixing.

Ask yourself: What system needs a tune-up you've been postponing?
