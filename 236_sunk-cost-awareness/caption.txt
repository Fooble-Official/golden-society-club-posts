Principle Nº 236: Sunk Cost Awareness

Don't throw good effort after bad. The money or time you've already spent is gone either way, it doesn't come back if you continue, and it doesn't get wasted retroactively if you stop.

Ask yourself: What are you continuing only because you've already invested, not because it works?
