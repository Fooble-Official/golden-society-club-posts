Principle Nº 237: Cultivate Auto-Pilot

Rely on systems, not motivation. Motivation is real, but it's also unreliable by design, it fluctuates with sleep, stress, and mood, which makes it a bad foundation for anything you need to happen consistently.

Ask yourself: Where are you still relying on motivation that systems should handle?
