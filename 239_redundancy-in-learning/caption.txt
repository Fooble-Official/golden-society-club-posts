Principle Nº 239: Redundancy in Learning

Multiple methods to encode knowledge. Reading something once creates recognition, not retention, you'll nod along if you see it again, but you probably can't produce it from scratch when you actually need it.

Ask yourself: What did you learn this week that you haven't reinforced through a second mode?
