Principle Nº 241: Simplify Everything

A business becomes easier to scale when it clearly understands what each core stakeholder wants. Simplify Everything means reducing the business to the real desires of the people involved.

Ask yourself: Have you actually asked your suppliers, partners, and customers what they want, or are you still guessing on their behalf?
