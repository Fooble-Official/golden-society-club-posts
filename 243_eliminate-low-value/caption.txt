Principle Nº 243: Eliminate Low Value

If removing something does not reduce the value of the business, it should probably be removed, delayed, or simplified. Eliminate Low Value is the discipline of testing whether something actually matters.

Ask yourself: What is the one thing, if you eliminated this, would no longer be valuable to you?
