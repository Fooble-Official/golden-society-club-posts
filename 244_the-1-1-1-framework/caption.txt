Principle Nº 244: The 1-1-1 Framework

Fast growth comes from simplifying the business around one traffic source, one conversion method, and one delivery channel before adding complexity. The 1-1-1 Framework protects you from trying to grow ten things before one thing works.

Ask yourself: What is the one traffic source, one conversion method, and one delivery channel you should commit to before adding anything else?
