Principle Nº 246: The Plumbing Principle

Fix the system before increasing the flow of leads, because scaling traffic through broken operations creates waste. The Plumbing Principle means traffic is not automatically good.

Ask yourself: Where would more leads currently create more problems instead of more profit?
