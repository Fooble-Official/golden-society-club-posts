Principle Nº 248: Growth By Subtraction

Scaling requires doing fewer things better, because complexity grows faster than revenue. Growth by Subtraction is counterintuitive because ambitious people often assume more effort equals more success.

Ask yourself: What should you subtract so the business can grow faster?
