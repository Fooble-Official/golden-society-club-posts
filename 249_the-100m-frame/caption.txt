Principle Nº 249: The 100M Frame

Asking what a $10M or $100M version of the business would require reveals which current systems are unsustainable. The 100M Frame helps you stop solving problems only from today’s perspective.

Ask yourself: What would the $10M or $100M version of this business refuse to keep doing?
