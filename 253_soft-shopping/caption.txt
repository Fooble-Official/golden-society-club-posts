Principle Nº 253: Soft Shopping

Testing buyer interest before you are ready to sell gives you the clearest business plan for increasing value. Soft Shopping means casually taking the business to market or to experienced buyers for feedback before you actually need to sell.

Ask yourself: If a buyer looked at your business today, what would stop them from paying a premium?
