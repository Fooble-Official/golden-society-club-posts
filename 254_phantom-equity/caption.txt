Principle Nº 254: Phantom Equity

Key employees can be motivated with phantom ownership that rewards them in a future sale without giving away actual equity. Phantom Equity gives someone the feeling of ownership economics without making them an actual owner.

Ask yourself: Who needs to feel economically aligned with the long-term value of this business?
