Principle Nº 255: Three Growth Paths

Growth can be funded through profits, outside capital, or employee ownership structures, and each path changes control, risk, and upside. There are usually three broad ways to fund growth.

Ask yourself: Which growth path best protects your control, speed, and long-term upside?
