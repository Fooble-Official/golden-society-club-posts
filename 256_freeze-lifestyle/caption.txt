Principle Nº 256: Freeze Lifestyle

Keeping personal expenses flat as income rises creates the financial freedom to take bigger strategic risks. Freeze Lifestyle means resisting lifestyle inflation.

Ask yourself: What personal expenses should stay flat so your future options can expand?
