Principle Nº 259: Second Order Thinking

Strong founders evaluate not only the immediate result of a decision, but also the consequences one, five, and ten years later. Second-order thinking means looking beyond the first obvious result.

Ask yourself: What are the second and third order consequences of the decision you are about to make?
