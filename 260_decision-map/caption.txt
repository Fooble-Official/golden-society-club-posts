Principle Nº 260: Decision Map

Better decisions come from understanding the facts, isolating the issue, accepting the risks, and mapping the next steps. The Decision Map gives structure to moments that feel messy.

Ask yourself: Which of these four, facts, issue, risk, or next step, are you most likely to skip when you're under pressure?
