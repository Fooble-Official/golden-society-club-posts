Principle Nº 264: Hire Pain Growth

Hire only to solve the pain of today or capture the growth of tomorrow. Hire Pain Growth keeps hiring disciplined.

Ask yourself: Are you hiring to relieve a real pain, capture a real opportunity, or avoid a responsibility you have not clarified?
