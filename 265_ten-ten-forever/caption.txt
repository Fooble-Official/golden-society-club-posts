Principle Nº 265: Ten Ten Forever

Wealth creation is often a who strategy, so invest deeply in a small group of people over a long period of time. The 10-10 Forever Rule says wealth is not only created through tactics.

Ask yourself: Who are the 10 people you should invest in for the next 10 years?
