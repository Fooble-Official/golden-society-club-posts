Principle Nº 266: Skill Stacking

Combining unrelated skills can create a unique advantage that competitors cannot easily copy. Skill Stacking means your edge may not come from being the best in the world at one thing.

Ask yourself: What combination of skills gives you an advantage no one else can easily replicate?
