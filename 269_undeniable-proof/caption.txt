Principle Nº 269: Undeniable Proof

The strongest persuasion removes the imagination gap by showing results people cannot ignore. Undeniable Proof closes the gap between what you know is true and what others can believe.

Ask yourself: What proof would make your value obvious without needing a long explanation?
