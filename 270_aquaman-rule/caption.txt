Principle Nº 270: Aquaman Rule

Real persistence means staying in the game even when you do not currently believe you are going to win. The Aquaman Rule says not giving up can be the most heroic thing you do.

Ask yourself: Where do you need to keep going even though you do not currently feel certain you will win?
