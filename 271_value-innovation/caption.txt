Principle Nº 271: Value Innovation

Pursue differentiation and low cost simultaneously to create a leap in buyer value. Cirque du Soleil eliminated star performers and animal acts (lower cost) while creating theatrical narrative and artistic music (higher value).

Ask yourself: Which factors should we eliminate, reduce, raise, and create to make competition irrelevant?
