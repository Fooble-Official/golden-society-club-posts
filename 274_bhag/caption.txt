Principle Nº 274: BHAG

Set a Big Hairy Audacious Goal that galvanizes the team for 10 to 30 years. NASA’s Apollo goal, land on the Moon by decade’s end, is the canonical BHAG.

Ask yourself: What is the 10-year goal so audacious that pursuing it would change how you run the company?
