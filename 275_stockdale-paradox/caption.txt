Principle Nº 275: Stockdale Paradox

Hold unwavering faith you will prevail while confronting the most brutal facts of your reality. Stockdale survived years of imprisonment because he refused both false optimism (those who believed they would be home by Christmas died of broken hearts) and despair.

Ask yourself: What brutal facts are you avoiding, and does your team know you still believe you will prevail?
