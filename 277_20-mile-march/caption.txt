Principle Nº 277: 20-Mile March

Hit consistent performance targets in good times and bad. Amundsen reached the South Pole by marching 15–20 miles daily regardless of weather; Scott raced when sunny and rested when bad and lost the race and his life.

Ask yourself: What is your non-negotiable monthly minimum, and have you hit it every month this year?
