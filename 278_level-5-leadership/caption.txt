Principle Nº 278: Level 5 Leadership

Combine personal humility with fierce professional will. Darwin Smith at Kimberly-Clark sold the paper mills (the company’s historic core) because the future was in consumer products.

Ask yourself: Are you leading with personal humility and professional will, or just one?
