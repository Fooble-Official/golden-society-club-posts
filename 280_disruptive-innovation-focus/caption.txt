Principle Nº 280: Disruptive Innovation Focus

Build products for low-end or non-consumption markets that incumbents ignore. Netflix’s DVD-by-mail looked terrible to Blockbuster (worse selection, longer wait).

Ask yourself: What underserved segment could you serve with a simpler, cheaper version while incumbents protect their margins?
