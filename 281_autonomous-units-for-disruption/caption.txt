Principle Nº 281: Autonomous Units for Disruption

Protect disruptive projects by isolating them from incumbent processes. IBM’s PC group succeeded because IBM let it operate outside corporate process.

Ask yourself: Is the disruptive project you care about being judged by metrics it cannot win on?
