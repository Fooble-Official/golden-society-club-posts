Principle Nº 283: Core Competencies

Build strategy on rare, hard-to-imitate, broadly applicable internal strengths. Honda’s engine design is the classic example: motorcycles, cars, lawn mowers, generators all leveraged the same root competence.

Ask yourself: What rare capability could you build deeply enough that it opens multiple markets?
