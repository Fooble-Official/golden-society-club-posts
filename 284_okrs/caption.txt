Principle Nº 284: OKRs

Align the organization around a few ambitious objectives, each with measurable key results. Google’s OKRs powered its early growth: bold objectives like "improve search quality" with hard KRs like "increase query-success rate." OKRs are not bonus systems, tying KRs to compensation kills stretch.

Ask yourself: What 3 objectives, with measurable key results, should you commit to publicly this quarter?
