Principle Nº 285: Porter’s Five Forces

Industry profitability is shaped by rivalry, new entrants, buyer power, supplier power, and substitutes. Telecom carriers face high rivalry and substitutes; they win by raising switching costs (contracts, bundled services).

Ask yourself: Which of the five forces is currently compressing your margins most, and what move would weaken it?
