Principle Nº 286: Pareto Principle

About 80 percent of results come from about 20 percent of causes. Pareto noticed 80% of Italy’s land was owned by 20% of people.

Ask yourself: Which 20% of your work produces 80% of your results, and are you protecting time for it?
