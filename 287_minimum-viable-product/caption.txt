Principle Nº 287: Minimum Viable Product

Ship the smallest version that lets you start the Build-Measure-Learn loop. Zappos started by photographing shoes at local stores and listing them online without holding inventory.

Ask yourself: What is the smallest thing I could ship this week that would tell me whether the riskiest assumption is true?
