Principle Nº 288: Build-Measure-Learn Loop

Iterate by building, measuring real behavior, and learning whether to pivot or persevere. The loop’s power is compounding learning.

Ask yourself: How fast is your Build-Measure-Learn cycle right now, and what is the next thing slowing it down?
