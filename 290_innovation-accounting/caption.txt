Principle Nº 290: Innovation Accounting

Track startup progress with metrics that reveal whether the business model is working. A startup with rising downloads but flat retention may look healthy and be dying.

Ask yourself: Are my metrics actionable cohort signals, or vanity numbers that hide stagnation?
