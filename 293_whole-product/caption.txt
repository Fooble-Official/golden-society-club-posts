Principle Nº 293: Whole Product

Mainstream customers buy the whole solution, not the core product. Enterprise software vendors learned to bundle implementation, training, and support because mainstream IT buyers will not assemble the whole product themselves.

Ask yourself: What does a customer need to succeed beyond my core product, and am I providing it?
