Principle Nº 294: Monopoly 10x Thinking

Aim for a 10x improvement that creates a monopoly position. PayPal’s online payment ease and security was so much better than checks for eBay sellers that it locked in a dominant position.

Ask yourself: On what one dimension can I credibly become 10x better than alternatives?
