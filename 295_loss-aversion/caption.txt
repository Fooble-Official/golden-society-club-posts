Principle Nº 295: Loss Aversion

People weigh losses roughly twice as much as equivalent gains. Kahneman’s prospect theory showed people refuse a coin-flip for "lose 100 or win 110" even though the expected value is positive.

Ask yourself: Am I framing my offer as preventing a loss customers feel, not just adding a gain?
