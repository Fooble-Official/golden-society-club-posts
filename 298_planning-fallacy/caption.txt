Principle Nº 298: Planning Fallacy

People consistently underestimate how long and costly tasks will be. Major infrastructure projects routinely come in late and over budget because planners use only inside-view estimates.

Ask yourself: What is the outside-view estimate from similar past projects, and how does it compare to my inside-view plan?
