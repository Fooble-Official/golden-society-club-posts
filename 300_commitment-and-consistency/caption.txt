Principle Nº 300: Commitment and Consistency

Small commitments create pressure to remain consistent with later, larger commitments. Studies show people who agreed to a small yard sign were far more likely later to accept a much larger one.

Ask yourself: What is the smallest aligned first commitment that would set users on the path to the larger commitment?
