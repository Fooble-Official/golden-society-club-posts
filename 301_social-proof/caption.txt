Principle Nº 301: Social Proof

People look to others when uncertain, especially others similar to themselves. Cialdini’s studies on energy conservation showed neighbors’ actual usage was the strongest motivator, stronger than environmental, financial, or moral framing.

Ask yourself: What specific social proof would reassure a prospect most similar to my ideal customer?
