Principle Nº 304: Scarcity

Items become more attractive when they appear rare or limited. Cialdini shows scarcity is a powerful motivator but only when authentic.

Ask yourself: Am I using genuine scarcity my customers can verify, or manufactured urgency that will erode trust?
