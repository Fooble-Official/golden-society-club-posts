Principle Nº 306: Adjacent Possible

Innovation grows from the immediate vicinity of current capabilities. Instagram emerged adjacent to existing photo apps by adding filters and a social feed.

Ask yourself: What is the adjacent possible move that uses what I already have to open something new?
