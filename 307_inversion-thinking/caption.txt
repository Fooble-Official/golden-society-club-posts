Principle Nº 307: Inversion Thinking

Solve problems by considering their opposite first, eliminate failure modes rather than chase success. Munger built Berkshire by avoiding stupidity rather than chasing brilliance.

Ask yourself: If this fails, what will the autopsy reveal, and have I addressed it now?
