Principle Nº 308: Founder-Market Fit

Founders win when their identity, experience, and obsessions align with the market they serve. Founder-Market fit explains why some founders out-execute despite weaker resumes: their visceral understanding shortens every learning loop.

Ask yourself: Why am I the right person to serve this market, and is the answer specific or generic?
