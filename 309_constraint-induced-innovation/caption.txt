Principle Nº 309: Constraint-Induced Innovation

Resource constraints can force creative breakthroughs that abundance prevents. Apple’s early garage origins, Twitter’s 140-character limit, Basecamp’s deliberate small-team constraint, each used limitation as design discipline.

Ask yourself: What unconventional solution would I find if I refused to fix this with more money or time?
