Principle Nº 310: Assumption Testing

Surface and test the riskiest hypotheses before committing major resources. Discovery-Driven Planning makes assumptions explicit and allocates spending based on what each milestone has validated.

Ask yourself: What is the riskiest assumption I am betting on, and what is the cheapest way to test it this week?
