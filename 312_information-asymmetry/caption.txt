Principle Nº 312: Information Asymmetry

In every transaction, one party has more information than the other. Akerlof showed that without trust signals, low-quality products drive out high-quality ones because buyers cannot tell the difference and refuse to pay premium prices.

Ask yourself: Where is information asymmetry causing my customers to hesitate, and what signal would close the gap?
