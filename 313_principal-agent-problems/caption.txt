Principle Nº 313: Principal-Agent Problems

Agents (employees, executives) may pursue their own interests when those diverge from principals (owners). Bonuses tied to top-line revenue produce sales reps who close bad-fit deals.

Ask yourself: Where are my team's actual incentives pulling them away from what the company needs?
