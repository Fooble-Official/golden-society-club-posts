Principle Nº 314: Lollapalooza Effect

When several psychological forces stack, outcomes become disproportionate to any single cause. Munger’s example: Coca-Cola’s success combined operant conditioning, social proof, authority, scarcity, and ritualistic behavior.

Ask yourself: What multiple forces could stack to produce a result much larger than any single tactic?
