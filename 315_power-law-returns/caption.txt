Principle Nº 315: Power Law Returns

In many markets a few outcomes dominate the rest by orders of magnitude. Sequoia’s top fund returns came from a handful of investments, most companies returned little or zero.

Ask yourself: Am I treating my portfolio of bets like a normal distribution when it is really a power law?
