Principle Nº 316: Goodhart’s Law

When a measure becomes a target, it ceases to be a good measure. Customer service teams measured on "tickets closed per hour" close tickets without solving problems.

Ask yourself: Which of my metrics has become a target, and what is the team doing now that misses the underlying goal?
