Principle Nº 317: Path Dependence

Today’s state is shaped by past decisions that constrain present options. QWERTY keyboard layout, US railroad gauge, and many tech-stack choices persist because switching costs exceed switching benefits.

Ask yourself: Which of my current constraints is leftover path dependence I could choose to unwind?
