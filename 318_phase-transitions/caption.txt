Principle Nº 318: Phase Transitions

Systems undergo abrupt, system-wide changes when parameters cross thresholds. Blockbuster’s collapse, Nokia’s smartphone miss, and the rise of mobile commerce were all phase transitions.

Ask yourself: What threshold is my market or company approaching where small changes could trigger a step shift?
