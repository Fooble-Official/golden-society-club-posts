Principle Nº 319: Red Queen Effect

In a co-evolving market, you must run faster just to stay in place. Nokia and Blockbuster both stopped running at market pace; Apple and Netflix kept accelerating.

Ask yourself: Am I improving at the speed of my market, or quietly falling behind?
