Principle Nº 320: Systems Thinking

Complex problems require seeing the whole system of interactions, not just isolated symptoms. Donella Meadows showed that the highest-leverage interventions are usually at paradigm or rule levels, not at parameter levels.

Ask yourself: What recurring problem in my business is being produced by the structure rather than by individual choices?
