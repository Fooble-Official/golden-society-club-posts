Principle Nº 324: Irreversibility

Decisions vary in how easily they can be reversed; treat them differently. Bezos argued most decisions in business are reversible (two-way doors) and should be made fast.

Ask yourself: Is this decision a one-way or two-way door, and am I treating it accordingly?
