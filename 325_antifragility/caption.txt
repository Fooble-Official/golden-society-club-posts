Principle Nº 325: Antifragility

Some systems get stronger from volatility instead of weaker. Restaurants run brittle (one bad month kills them); R&D labs run antifragile (most experiments fail, but the rare hits more than compensate).

Ask yourself: Where in my business does volatility hurt me, and where could I redesign so it helps?
