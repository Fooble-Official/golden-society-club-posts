Principle Nº 327: Ergodicity

Time averages and ensemble averages are not the same, protect against ruin. A coin-flip with double-or-zero has positive expected value but guarantees ruin if repeated.

Ask yourself: Am I taking any bet whose worst outcome would actually ruin me?
