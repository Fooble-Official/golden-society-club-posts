Principle Nº 328: Organizational Inertia

Established organizations resist change even when change is clearly needed. Studies show 68–87% of leaders identify organizational inertia as the top barrier to transformation.

Ask yourself: On my biggest needed change, am I relying on persuasion when I should be redesigning structure?
